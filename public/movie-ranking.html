The Best Movies According to Avantika

My top 9 Movies of all time

Fidaa

This is just the best. the music, the aesthetic, the actors and the story is just really heartwarming.

Taqdeer

I have watched it so many times - I dont even have the count. The songs here are so amazing - I love it. I love the actors and though it seems a bit too much specially the childhood part, it is still a wonderful feel-good movie.

Cinderalla

Of course, it is a classic fairytale story but I love it. The ball dance and the music is my favourite of all time.

La La Land

I just recently watched it. no doubt, it won the oscar because it truly is a masterpiece. I love Emma Stone so much.

Black Widow

I just love it. Seeing Natasha and all the widows whooping everybody's asses. No offense. The female representaion in action is awesome here and I love it.

Avatar: Way of Water

Well, this is my comfort movie - the ocean, the na'avi people, the animals, the forest, the trees, the sea creatures, everything is just so so good. I love it. I love those people and their bonds with the environment.

10 Things I Hate About You

Wow, wow and wow. The poem - I loved it so much that I had memorized it. Love and respect for Heath Ledger.

Superman Returns

This is my favourite Superman movie of all time. The dynamics between Lois and Superman are just so intense and amazing. And I love it. Even the potrayal of Superman is so apt. This is just an underrated movie.

Extraction

Chris Hemsworth is absolutely amazing in this movie. WOW. This increases the action standard for a movie.
